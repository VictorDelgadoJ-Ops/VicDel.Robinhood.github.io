About Robinhood

Learn about Robinhood’s mission, history, and role in democratizing finance.

Robinhood was founded in 2013 with the goal of making investing accessible to everyone. Its commission‑free model disrupted traditional brokerage firms and encouraged millions of new investors to enter the market.
