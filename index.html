Robinhood: A beginner-friendly trading app

Explore how Robinhood works, what it offers, and the considerations to keep in mind. This page is educational and does not provide financial advice.

Overview

Robinhood is a mobile-first platform for trading and investing. It offers commission-free trading for certain products, simple interfaces, and basic tools aimed at accessibility. Users can view market data, place orders, and manage accounts from the app or web.

Purpose

Provide a streamlined way to buy and sell eligible financial instruments with a focus on ease of use.

Audience

Retail investors seeking a simplified experience, with options to learn and participate in markets.

Availability

Accessible via iOS/Android apps and a web platform; account eligibility and features vary by region.

Features

Common capabilities you may find in the app. Specific availability can change over time.

Trading products

Stocks, ETFs, options, and cryptocurrencies (availability and restrictions may apply). Fractional shares are supported for select equities.

Account types

Standard brokerage accounts, with optional features such as margin (subject to eligibility) and premium subscriptions.

Tools & research

Watchlists, basic charting, news integrations, alerts, and educational content within the app.

User experience

Mobile-first design, quick onboarding, and streamlined order placement flows with simplified settings.

How it works

A typical user journey from sign-up to placing a trade.

1. Sign up & verify

Create an account, complete identity verification, and connect a funding source (e.g., bank account) to deposit funds.

2. Explore and learn

Browse watchlists, read basic educational materials, and review disclosures related to products and risks.

3. Place orders

Select an instrument and choose order types (market, limit, etc.). Review details before submitting.

4. Manage & monitor

Track positions, view performance, set alerts, and adjust your approach as you learn.

Fees and costs

Commission-free trading may apply to certain products, but other fees can exist.
