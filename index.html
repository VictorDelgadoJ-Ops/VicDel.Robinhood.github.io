Overview of Robinhood

Robinhood offers commission-free trades but may include other costs. Trading involves risk, and strategies can magnify losses.

Features

  • Commission-free trading for stocks, ETFs, and options
  • Premium $5/month for Robinhood Gold
  • Encryption and SIPC protection

Risks

Market volatility, leverage (margin), and options strategies can magnify losses. SIPC covers brokerage failure, not market losses.

FAQs

Is Robinhood free?

Yes, trades are commission-free, but other fees may apply.

Does Robinhood protect my investments?

SIPC covers brokerage failure, but not market losses.
