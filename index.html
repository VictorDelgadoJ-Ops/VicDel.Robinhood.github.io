Learn About Robinhood

Understand features, fees, and risks before investing.

Overview

Robinhood is a mobile-first platform for trading and investing.

Purpose

Commission-free trading with a focus on ease of use.

Audience

Retail investors seeking a simplified experience.

Availability

Accessible via iOS/Android apps and web platform.

Features

Trading products

Stocks, ETFs, options, and cryptocurrencies.

Account types

Standard brokerage accounts, margin, premium subscriptions.

Tools & research

Watchlists, charts, news, alerts, education.

Fees

Robinhood offers commission-free trades but may include other costs.

Fee TypeDetails
TradingCommission-free for stocks, ETFs, options.
Premium$5/month for Robinhood Gold.
OtherRegulatory fees, margin interest, transfer fees.

Security

Robinhood uses encryption, two-factor authentication, and SIPC insurance to protect user assets.

Risks

While Robinhood simplifies investing, it may encourage impulsive trading. Market volatility and lack of education can lead to losses.

FAQs

Is Robinhood free?

Yes, trades are commission-free, but other fees may apply.

Is Robinhood safe?

Robinhood uses encryption and insurance, but investing always carries risk.

Can I trade crypto?

Yes, Robinhood supports select cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin and Ethereum.
