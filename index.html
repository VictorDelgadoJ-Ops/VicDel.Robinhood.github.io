Learn About Robinhood

Understand features, fees, and risks before investing.

Overview

Robinhood is a mobile-first platform for trading and investing.

Purpose

Commission-free trading with ease of use.

Audience

Retail investors seeking a simplified experience.

Availability

Accessible via iOS/Android apps and web platform.

Features

Trading Products

Stocks, ETFs, options, and cryptocurrencies.

Account Types

Standard brokerage, margin, premium subscriptions.

Tools & Research

Watchlists, charts, news, alerts, education.

Fees

Robinhood offers commission-free trades but has other costs.

Fee TypeDetails
TradingCommission-free for stocks, ETFs, options.
Premium$5/month for Robinhood Gold.
OtherRegulatory fees, margin interest.

Security

Robinhood uses encryption, 2FA, and SIPC insurance.

Risks

Investing involves risk. Robinhood’s simplicity can encourage risky behavior.

FAQs

Is Robinhood free?

Yes, trades are commission-free, but other fees may apply.

Is Robinhood safe?

It uses encryption and insurance, but market risks remain.
