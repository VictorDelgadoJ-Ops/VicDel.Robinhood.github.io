Overview

Robinhood is a mobile-first platform for trading and investing.

Purpose

Commission-free trading with a focus on ease of use.

Audience

Retail investors seeking a simplified experience.

Availability

Accessible via iOS/Android apps and web platform.

Features

Trading products

Stocks, ETFs, options, and cryptocurrencies.

Account types

Standard brokerage, margin accounts, and premium subscriptions.

Tools & research

Watchlists, charts, news, alerts, and in-app education.

Interface

Clean, mobile-first design with streamlined onboarding and trading flows.

Fees

Robinhood offers commission-free trades but may include other costs.

Fee typeDetails
TradingCommission-free for stocks, ETFs, and options.
Premium$5/month for Robinhood Gold.
OtherRegulatory fees, margin interest, and transfer fees may apply.

Security

Robinhood implements encryption, optional two-factor authentication, and SIPC protection. Remember, SIPC covers brokerage failure, not market losses.

Risks

Investing involves risk. A simplified interface can encourage frequent trading. Market volatility, leverage (margin), and options strategies can magnify losses.

FAQs

Is Robinhood free?

Trades are commission-free, but premium subscriptions and regulatory fees may apply.

Is Robinhood safe?

It uses encryption and offers 2FA; assets are covered by SIPC for brokerage failure. Market risk still applies.

Can I trade crypto?

Yes. Support for select cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin and Ethereum varies by region.
